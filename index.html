About Me

My name is Nicole Pelletier and I take advantage of every opportunity to see animals. Dogs are my favorite animal because of their intelligence and loyalty. I first got a dog when I was seven years old. Lucie, a Cocker Spaniel-Poodle mix came into my family in 2007. Since then, I have met dogs in my travels to Central America and through the foster program at my local Humane Society.
